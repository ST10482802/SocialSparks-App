# ✨ The Social Sparks App

An amiable Android software that, depending on the time of day, recommends modest social activities#Qualities

Enter the time of day (morning, afternoon, evening, or night).
Receive a considerate social recommendation
To clear everything, use the reset button.
- Helpful error messages# How to Apply

1. Launch the app
2. Enter the time: night, dinner, morning, or afternoon.
3. Press "Get Spark"

Cora: to keep social ties strong during hectic days.# Made With
